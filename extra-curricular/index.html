Extra-curriculars

Student Committee member of a student run maker space. Role included inventory management for projects and ensuring maintenance of equipment and machinery like 3D printers, laser cutters, plasma cutters etc. Allocation and management of an annual budget as a part of the student committee.




Employment Operations, Nirmaan

Nirmaan is a non profit organisation to help the children and adults in the slum areas of Zari and Lamani. I've contributed to helping the women in these slums find livelihood by helping them work on skill-building products and monetize the products. I've also volunteered in teaching and holding workshops for kids in these slums. The picture on the left is of a sanitation workshop in one of Zari's schools.
